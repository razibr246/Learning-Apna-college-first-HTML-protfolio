Chocolate Pancake Recipe

These soft and fluffy chocolate pancakes are perfect for breakfast or dessert. They are easy to make and taste delicious with syrup or fresh fruits.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour, cocoa powder, and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add the egg and milk, then stir until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan and pour small amounts of batter onto it.
  4. Cook each pancake for 2 minutes on both sides.
  5. Serve warm with syrup or fruits.
